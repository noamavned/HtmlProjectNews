Self-Driving Taxi Goes Rogue, Drives Into Mall Fountain

A software malfunction causes an autonomous vehicle to take an unexpected detour

Shoppers at Lakeside Mall witnessed an unusual scene as an autonomous taxi rolled directly into the central fountain.

According to DriveSmart, the company behind the vehicle, a “map sync error” caused the taxi to deviate from its planned path.

Witnesses say the car slowly crossed a walkway before descending into the water.

Fortunately, no passengers were inside, and no injuries were reported.

DriveSmart has paused all operations while engineers investigate the malfunction.
